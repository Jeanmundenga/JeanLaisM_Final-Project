The Mona Lisa is an oil painting by Italian artist, inventor, and writer Leonardo da Vinci. Likely completed in 1506, the piece features a portrait of a seated woman set against an imaginary landscape. In addition to being one of the most famous paintings, it is also the most valuable.

Mona Lisa

Artiste: Leonardo da Vinci
Year: 1503-1506
Dimension: 77cm x 52cm
